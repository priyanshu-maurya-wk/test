Common Questions

FAQ

How long does a bespoke commission take?

Bespoke commissions typically take between 3 and 6 months from design approval to delivery. The precise timeline depends on the complexity of the piece and the series it draws from. During this time, we provide regular photographic updates from the atelier.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes. Every Noir Vessels piece is shipped via dedicated white-glove courier service with full insurance and climate-controlled packaging. We ship to 38 countries. For destinations not listed, please contact us directly — we will make arrangements.

What is included with each purchase?

Each piece is accompanied by a wax-sealed provenance document listing all artisans who worked on it, a cotton-lined ebony presentation box, a care guide printed on archival paper, and a personal note from the maison director. Limited editions also include a signed edition certificate.

Can I visit the atelier before purchasing?

Private atelier visits are available by appointment. We welcome collectors, press, and serious prospective buyers to witness the craft firsthand in Paris or Murano. Use the contact form above to request a visit and we will arrange a private appointment.

How do I care for my Noir Vessels piece?

Never place a Noir Vessels vessel in a dishwasher. Clean with a soft, damp cloth and a few drops of mild soap. For crystal pieces, a lint-free cloth ensures streak-free clarity. Keep out of direct sunlight for extended periods to protect colour treatments. Full care instructions are included with every piece.

Is the glass safe for use with beverages?

Yes. All Noir Vessels glass is lead-free and food-safe. Our crystal is produced to EU Regulation 84/500/EEC standards. External decorations such as gold leaf and engravings do not come into contact with the interior. Each piece is individually tested before shipment.
