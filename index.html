Signature Series

The Noir Universe

  • The Obsidian Series

    Forged from volcanic basalt glass sourced from Sicily, each Obsidian vessel carries the raw energy of elemental origins. Hand-finished with platinum detailing over 60 hours of meticulous work.

  • Crystal Reserve

    Each Crystal Reserve piece is mouth-blown by master glassmakers in Bohemia using a 400-year-old technique. No two are identical — the natural variations in crystal density create ever-shifting light refractions.

  • Gold Meridian

    24-karat gold leaf is hand-applied using centuries-old gilding techniques by our Florentine artisans. The amber glass base is shaped over two days in our Murano atelier before the gilding process begins.

  • Amethyst Heritage

    Inspired by Art Nouveau jewellery, the Amethyst Heritage series combines deep violet blown glass with a sterling silver cage handcrafted by Parisian silversmiths in our 16th arrondissement atelier.

  • Travel Flask Collection

    Engineered for the discerning traveller. A slim silhouette in hand-stitched Italian leather, concealing a double-walled glass interior that maintains temperature for 18 hours. TSA-approved without compromise.

Our Philosophy

Obsession
Made Visible

Every vessel that leaves our atelier has been touched by at least forty pairs of hands over hundreds of hours. We do not manufacture — we sculpt time itself into tangible beauty.

Our master glassblowers train for a decade before they are permitted to shape a Noir Vessels piece. Each signature — a faint engraved numeral on the base — tells the story of who breathed life into that particular vessel.
